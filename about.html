About the artist

My name is Oleksandra Kaftanatii. I'm studying course BA Animation and Illustration at Anglia Ruskin university. I like experimenting with different things, mostly related to art, and trying to step outside my comfort zone. Besides drawing, I like skeletons, collecting books and others stuff, also like to watch horror and thrillers movies. I also like to write all kinds of stories and plan to release a comic book or my own indie animation with my stories sometime in the future, after I gain enough experience in the animation industry.
