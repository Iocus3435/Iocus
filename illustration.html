"Before the storm"- project 2025

This is a Ukrainian charity zine about bright memories from life before the storm. In this project, we had to draw our good memories before the war in Ukraine began in 2022. I drew my memory of music school, where I learned to play the cello. It is not a happy memory, but it taught me that despite difficult times, I must cherish every hour before losing them and living with regret in the future.

Invasion on Mariupol 2022

It was a project for my college, Coleg Ceredigion-Creative Arts level 2. I decided to create a small series of illustrations showing my experience when Russia invaded my city Mariupol on 24 February 2022. It wasn't easy for me at the time to draw and remember everything that my parents and I had gone through, but I decided that it was better to share what I knew and had experienced so that other people could understand the pain and fear of war and losing everything we had.
